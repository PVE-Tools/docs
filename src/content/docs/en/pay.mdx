<details>
<summary>View after-sales terms</summary>

- **Not covered**: Self-modified configs, unknown software, other scripts after service; hardware failures, power outages, force majeure; failure to follow instructions

**In short: if you don't touch what I fixed, I'm responsible for 7 days; if you break it yourself, please reorder.**
